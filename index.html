Municipality Emails

Municipality Email Maps

Interactive maps showing email infrastructure of ~15,300 municipalities in Germany, Austria, and Switzerland, based on public DNS records and network signals.

📨 Email Providers

Which provider handles each municipality's official email? Classified by jurisdiction: US cloud (Microsoft, Google, AWS), domestic, or foreign.

🛡️ Email Security

How well are municipality email domains protected against spoofing? Based on SPF and DMARC configuration.

Part of an ongoing research project currently in peer review. Open source & open data on GitHub.
